About Us

Our Project

The Canine Skin Detection system is a cutting-edge tool designed to assist pet owners and veterinarians in identifying common skin conditions in dogs. Utilizing advanced artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms, our system analyzes photos and videos of dogs to detect potential skin issues with high accuracy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower dog owners and veterinary professionals with the tools they need to identify and address skin conditions in dogs swiftly. We believe that early detection is key to ensuring the health and well-being of dogs, and our platform is designed to make that process as straightforward and accurate as possible.

Through continuous research and development, we aim to expand the capabilities of our system to cover a broader range of conditions and provide even more precise diagnoses.
