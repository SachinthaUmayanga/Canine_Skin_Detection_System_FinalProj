Common Dog Skin Conditions

Allergic Dermatitis

Allergic dermatitis is a common condition caused by allergens such as food, pollen, or flea bites. It can lead to intense itching, redness, and inflammation.

Hot Spots

Hot spots are red, moist, and irritated areas of skin often caused by constant licking or scratching. They can appear suddenly and spread quickly.

Ringworm

Despite its name, ringworm is a fungal infection. It appears as circular patches of hair loss with a red ring around them and can be highly contagious.

Mange

Mange is caused by mites that burrow into a dog's skin. It can lead to severe itching, hair loss, and secondary infections if left untreated.

Dandruff

Dandruff in dogs can be caused by dry skin, allergies, or poor diet. It appears as white flakes in the fur and can sometimes be accompanied by itching.

Fungal Infections

Fungal infections can affect the skin, causing redness, itching, and flaking. Some common fungi include yeast and ringworm.
